		Two ships of the United States Navy have been named American. 

USS American (1861), part of the Stone Fleet sunk at Charleston, South Carolina. Some references to this ship name it as the America
USS American (ID-2292), was a freighter built in 1900 and acquired by the US Navy early in 1918 and returned to her original owners in 1919
